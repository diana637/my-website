4. Healthy Lifestyle

In this section you will find tips to improve your well-being. This segment focuses on key points that contribute to a healthy and balanced life, including food, mental health, stress and sleep management. By following these points, you can enhance your physical health.

  • Healthy Food

    • Having a balanced diet is crucial in supporting a healthy lifestyle. Let's discover the benefits and the best ingredients that contribute to a healthy lifestyle.

    • Benefits:
      • Weight loss
      • High energy
      • Reduce the chance of disease
      • Supports overall well-being

        • Meat: chicken, beef
        • Mixed nuts
        • Fruits: berries, mango, banana, apple
        • Vegetables: carrots, broccoli, salad, spinach
        • Fish: white fish
        • Olive Oil
        • Rice
        • Natural Yogurt

    • Meditation

      • Meditation is a practice activity to reduce stress, improve emotional well-being and enhance attention. To start meditation is important to consider a quiet room, get comfortable, start with a short time limit and increase the time every session, and bring your attention on your breath. There are so many meditation videos where the instructor provide instructions that helps you to stay focus.

      Make your own schedule so you can practice regularly and be patient because this activity takes time to develop.

    • Stress Management

      • It is crucial to learn how to manage stress for keeping mental and physical health.

      Here are some techniques:

      • Get enough sleep during the night
      • Keep practicing meditation
      • Outdoor Running
      • Eat Healthy
      • Be mindful
      • Practice deep breathing
      • Ask for help when you need it
      • Plan your day
      • Read your favorite book/ article
      • Focus on things that makes you happy

    • Sleep

      • Sleep refreshes your mind, is essential for health and wellbeing, and it supports your memory.

      Techniques for a good sleep:

      • Regulate a sleep schedule
      • Practice calming activities such as having a cup of calming tea (lavender, chamomile, lemon, mint) before bedtime, reading a book
      • Pay attention to room temperature, turn off the lights so you can make your room dark
      • Reduce exposure to any devices
      • Avoid heavy foods before bedtime and hydrated during the day
      • Outside activities such as walking, gardening, running, or just enjoy the sunlight (when is warm)
