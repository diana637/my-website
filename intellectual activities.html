3. Intellectual Activities

    BRAIN GAMES

    Engaging in brain games is a perfect way to enhance cognitive functions, improve you problem-solving skills and keep your mind active. Brain games are games that challenge your mental potentiality. These games helps to keep your brain active and provide mental stimulation. The activities can be fun and rewarding, and you can play them with family or friends.

    Benefits:
    • Improve your memory
    • Develop attention to details
    • Improve Problem-Solving skills
    • Help your Wellbeing

    BOOK CLUB

      A Book Club is a excellent way to interact with literature, and to engage with others who share a passion for reading. A book club activity can improve your reading and wellbeing, and also help you to engage in the community for discussion.

      Benefits:

      • Gives you the motivation to read regularly
      • Book Clubs is one of the best way to meet new people and to interact with them
      • Gives you the chance to engage in discussion and analysis about books which enhances critical thinking

      How to Join a Book Club

      • Look for Book Clubs
      • Select the books
      • Decide the meeting schedule with all members
      • Prepare discussion and share your thoughts

      Examples of Book Clubs Activities

      • Regular Discussion about the book
      • Learn more about the authors
      • Reading Challenges/Goals for reading
