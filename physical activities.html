2. Physical Activities
  • Outdoor Running Tips and Advanges

    • Whether you're a beginner or a experienced runner, our tips will help you make best of your outdoor runs. Let's learn how to stay safe with our advice of running, stay confident and motivated.

    [4] unsplash

  • TIPS:
    • Right Footwear- Invest in a good pair of running shoes to provide your foot proper support and comfort.
    • Pre-run Preparation- Include exercises like high knees or leg swing to prepare your muscles for the run.

    • Plan your Route- Make sure you run in a familiar area so that will be easier and safe for you to follow.
    • Drink Water- Start hydrating properly before running, and carry a water bottle so you can have acces to it during and after your route.

      • [6] unsplash

    • Wearing- Dress appropriately depending on weather. If is cold dress in layers and when is hot make sure you have a hat and suncream.
    • Posture- Head up, back straight, and shoulders relaxed.
    • Ensure your safety- Pay attention to any signs of pain. Take a break when needed.

  • ADVANGES:

    • Fresh Air- Breathing fresh air when running can improve your wellbeing.
    • Vitamin D- Regular exposure to sunlight helps your body synthesise vitamin D, which contribute to bone health.
    • Burns Calories- The more you run, the more calories you will burn
    • Connection with nature- Outdoor running makes you feel connected with the nature and it can reduce stress, depression, and anxiety.

    • Home Workout Routines

      • With busy schedules, most of us are using home workout routines to maintain our fitness and health. Home workout offer flexibility, is comfortable and maintain the privacy.

      Here are the steps on how to start home work routine:

      • Set your goals: loose weight, increase flexibility, build muscle
      • Choose your exercises
      • Create a Schedule: decide how many days per week you want to work out
      • Find your place: living, bedroom, garden

      Exercise types:

    • Squats
    • Crunches
    • Pull-ups
    • Push-ups
    • Leg raises

      • Benefits:

    • Cost free, you can exercise anytime outside or inside in your home
    • Private environment, and comfortable
    • Family members can join you anytime
    • It give you the flexibility, also is easy to maintain a regular workout routine.
